## Key Concepts to Master

### AI Development Options
| Option | Best For | Expertise Needed |
|--------|----------|------------------|
| Pre-trained APIs | Common tasks (vision, NLP, speech) | Low |
| AutoML | Custom models without coding | Low-Medium |
| Custom Training | Full control over model architecture | High |
| BigQuery ML | SQL-based ML | Medium |

### ML Workflow Stages
1. **Data Preparation**: Collect, clean, transform data
2. **Model Development**: Train, tune, evaluate models
3. **Model Serving**: Deploy, monitor, update models

### Generative AI vs Predictive AI
| Aspect | Predictive AI | Generative AI |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Output | Predictions/classifications | New content |
| Examples | Spam detection, recommendations | Text generation, image creation |
| Training | Labeled data | Large unlabeled datasets |
