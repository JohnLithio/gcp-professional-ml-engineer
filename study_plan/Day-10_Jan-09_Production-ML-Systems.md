## Hardware Selection Guide

| Use Case | Recommended Hardware |
|----------|---------------------|
| Small models, inference | CPU |
| DNN training, parallel ops | GPU (T4, V100, A100) |
| Large TensorFlow models | TPU v4 |
| Edge/mobile | Edge TPU, TF Lite |
